## Overview
**MealMate** is a browser-native web application designed to help individuals and households plan their weekly meals, manage their grocery shopping, and reduce food waste through smart pantry tracking.

## Problem Statement
Many people struggle to answer the daily question: *"What should I eat this week?"* Without a plan, they tend to over-buy groceries, forget what they already have at home, and waste both food and money. MealMate solves this by providing an integrated tool that connects recipe browsing, weekly meal planning, grocery list generation, and pantry management in one place.

## Target Group
- **Primary:** University students and young professionals living independently who need to manage a tight weekly food budget.
- **Secondary:** Families and households looking to reduce food waste and spend less time deciding what to cook.

## Application Value
| Feature | Benefit |
|---|---|
| Recipe Library with 15 real recipes | Immediate inspiration with no setup required |
| Diet tag filtering (vegan, gluten-free, etc.) | Suits diverse dietary needs |
| Weekly Meal Planner (drag & drop) | Structured planning for the whole week |
| Auto-generated Grocery List | Eliminates manual list-making |
| Pantry Deduction | Only buy what you don't already have |
| Weekly Budget Tracker | Stay within your food budget |
| 404 Page with navigation | Polished, professional user experience |
